My Two Favorite Places

Everyone has special places that hold happy memories. For me, the two best places I have ever visited are Everland amusement park and a cozy library, each offering a different kind of adventure. While one is full of exciting sounds and the other is calm and quiet, both are my favorite places to be.

My first favorite place is Everland, which is full of fantastic attractions. The most amazing feature of the park is its collection of animals. I loved observing the pandas, and I was happy to see curious monkeys and interesting snakes. The most memorable moment was a personal one; a beautiful butterfly landed right on my nose and tickled me! Besides the animals, the food was a wonderful part of the experience. My family and I shared some delicious popcorn that tasted very sweet. The combination of amazing animals and tasty treats makes Everland a truly joyful place.

My second favorite place is a large library on the top floor of an apartment building. At first, I was worried I wouldn't be able to find a book just for me among so many choices. I kept searching the shelves, hoping to locate the perfect story. Finally, I found a wonderful book about cats! I love cats because their fur is very soft and smooth. While I chose my cat book, my brother chose one about fish. The feeling of finding the exact book I wanted was a quiet and happy adventure, making the library my second best place.

In conclusion, both the thrilling excitement of Everland and the quiet discovery of the library are special to me. They show that a great place can be loud and busy or calm and peaceful. I cherish the different memories I made at both locations and hope to visit them again soon.
