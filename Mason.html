Part C: Writing Corrections

Sentence: "I'm gone a tel a story about where was the best place for me."

Correction:

I'm going to tell a story about the best place for me.

'~할 것이다'는 'I'm going to'라고 표현해요. '어디'라는 뜻의 'where'는 필요 없어요. 'tel a'는 'tell a'로 고쳤어요.

Suggested Improvement (G2):

I am going to tell you a story about my favorite place.

'the best place for me'보다 'my favorite place(내가 가장 좋아하는 장소)'라고 하면 더 자연스러워요.

Sentence: "From my opinion I is good and exellent!"

Correction:

In my opinion, it is good and excellent!

'나의 의견으로는'은 'In my opinion'이라고 해요. 주어 'I' 대신, 앞에서 말한 것을 가리키는 'it'을 써야 해요. 'I' 다음에는 'am'이 와야 하고, 'is'가 올 수 없어요.

Suggested Improvement (G2):

I think going on trips with my family is an excellent and good experience.

무엇이 좋고 훌륭한지('가족과 여행 가는 것')를 문장에 넣어주면 의미가 훨씬 명확해져요.

Sentence: "last, I loved went In went ilbon. I went to pokemon center, and bought lot of pokemon cards."

Correction:

Last, I loved when I went to Japan. I went to the Pokemon Center and bought a lot of Pokemon cards.

의미가 분명하도록 문장을 정리했어요. 'ilbon'은 일본의 영어 표기인 'Japan'으로 고쳤고, 'a lot of'라고 써야 해요.

Suggested Improvement (G2):

Finally, I will talk about my trip to Japan. I loved visiting the Pokemon Center because I bought many cool Pokemon cards.

'마지막으로, 일본 여행에 대해 이야기할게요'라고 시작하면 글의 흐름이 자연스러워져요. 'because'를 사용해서 포켓몬 센터가 왜 좋았는지 이유를 설명해주면 더 좋아요.

My Favorite Trip to Japan

(Introduction) A powerful first sentence grabs the reader's attention. I have visited many places, but my trip to Japan with my family was the best. I think Japan is a great place to visit because you can see amazing characters, eat delicious food, and enjoy fun games.

(Body Paragraph 1) First, I loved Japan because I could visit the Pokemon Center. I am a big fan of Pokemon, so it was very exciting. The store was very big and colorful. I bought a lot of cool Pokemon cards to add to my collection. I also saw a fun game machine called "Ga-Olé" that my friend told me about.

(Body Paragraph 2) Next, going on trips with my mom and dad is always excellent. In Japan, we went to many places together. We walked around the city and saw many interesting things. My parents always make our trips fun. It is great to explore new places like Japan with my family.

(Conclusion) In conclusion, Japan was the best place I have ever visited. I especially liked the Pokemon Center and buying cards. It was a very fun and excellent trip with my family. I hope I can go on more trips in the future.

다음 글을 쓸 때는, 짧은 문장이라도 괜찮으니 '누가? 무엇을 한다.' 이 두 가지를 꼭 넣어서 문장의 집을 지어보는 연습을 해보세요. 예를 들어, 'I went to Japan.' / 'My friend likes games.' 처럼요. 이렇게 튼튼한 집을 하나씩 짓다 보면, 곧 여러 집을 연결해서 멋진 마을(에세이)을 만들 수 있을 거예요. 선생님이 항상 응원할게요!
