Step 2: 어휘 선택 및 문장 흐름 교정

논리적 혼란 (Logical Confusion):** 두 번째 본론의 내용이 매우 혼란스럽습니다. "The city that have many people' world is the famous word, so American's word will be the word."라는 문장은 문법적으로도 틀렸을 뿐만 아니라, 무슨 의미를 전달하려는지 파악하기가 불가능합니다. 아이디어를 명확하게 표현하는 능력이 부족합니다.

제안 (Suggestion):** 글을 쓰기 전에, 각 문단에서 하고 싶은 말을 한국어로 먼저 한 문장으로 명확하게 정리하는 연습이 필요합니다. 그리고 그 문장을 가장 단순하고 쉬운 영어 문장으로 바꾸는 훈련을 해야 합니다. 복잡한 생각을 한 번에 영어로 옮기려고 하기보다, 생각을 단순화하는 과정이 중요합니다.

The Benefits of Learning English

Learning English has many important benefits. Two of these benefits are that it helps us understand things when we travel and allows us to talk with many people around the world.

First, understanding English is a big help in new places. If you don't know English, you can't read many signs or understand information, especially in a country like America. For example, if you are in a movie theater, you need to read the signs to know where your seat is. Knowing English helps you find your way.

Second, English allows you to talk with people from all over the world. English is the most popular language, so many people can speak it. This means you can make friends with people from different countries and learn about their cultures. It helps everyone to communicate and understand each other better.

In conclusion, learning English has many advantages. The most important ones are being able to read and understand information and being able to communicate with people globally. If you know English, you can learn more and connect with the world.
