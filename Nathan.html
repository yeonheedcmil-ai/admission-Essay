Part 4: 상세 교정 (Essay Corrections)

Step 1: 문장별 문법 및 철자 교정

원본 문장 교정 문장 친절한 설명
Just I think it is first , Learn ABCs... First, I think learning English starts with the ABCs... 문장 시작에 'Just'는 어색합니다. 'First'를 사용하여 순서를 나타내는 것이 좋습니다.
every one people of India, china... everyone from countries like India, China... 'every one people'은 틀린 표현입니다. 또한 'China'와 같은 국가는 대문자로 시작해야 합니다.
And it is good for the reading. In addition, it is helpful for reading. 'the reading'에서 관사 'the'는 생략하며, 'good' 대신 'helpful'을 쓰면 더 전문적입니다.
Hawaii that useing the english. Hawaii, where people use English. 'useing'은 'using'의 철자 오류이며, 문장이 동사를 갖춘 완전한 형태여야 합니다.
good to talk it with other. good to talk with others. 'talk it'보다는 'talk with'가 자연스럽고, '다른 사람들'은 'others'라고 표현합니다.

Step 2: 어휘 선택 및 문장 흐름 교정

  • 단어 반복: "English is good", "it is good"이 너무 많이 반복됩니다. "Learning English provides many advantages" 또는 "It is a powerful tool" 등으로 다양하게 표현해 보세요.
  • 연결어 부족: 문단 사이의 연결이 갑작스럽습니다. "Furthermore"(게다가), "For these reasons"(이러한 이유로)와 같은 연결어를 사용하면 글의 흐름이 훨씬 부드러워집니다.

Step 3: 최종 교정 에세이 (Final Model Essay)

Have you ever wondered why so many people around the world learn English? I believe that learning English is essential because it allows us to communicate with global citizens and helps us navigate our daily lives more easily.

First of all, English is the most effective tool for communication. It is used by people in many countries such as India, China, the U.S.A., and Italy. By learning English, we can make friends from different cultures and share our ideas with everyone. This makes the world feel much smaller and more connected.

Furthermore, English is incredibly useful for practical activities like reading and traveling. For instance, when you travel to Hawaii or the U.S.A., most signs and instructions are written in English. If you can read English, you can find your way and buy things without any trouble. Therefore, knowing English gives us the confidence to explore new places around the world.

In conclusion, learning English is beneficial for both communication and practical life. It opens up new opportunities and allows us to talk with others everywhere. For these reasons, I think everyone should enjoy learning this global language.

To. 민영 (Nathan)
