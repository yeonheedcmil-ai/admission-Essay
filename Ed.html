2. Student's Level Assessment

Vocabulary

Beginner (G2)

사용한 단어들은 대부분 G2 수준에 맞춰져 있어요. 'beach', 'hotel', 'family'와 같은 기본 단어들을 잘 사용하고 있고, 'delicious', 'snorkling' 같은 구체적인 단어도 사용하려는 좋은 시도가 보여요. 하지만 'good', 'fun', 'very' 같은 단어가 너무 자주 반복되어 글이 단조롭게 느껴질 수 있어요.
제안: 'good' 대신에 'wonderful(멋진)', 'enjoyable(즐거운)', 'pleasant(쾌적한)' 같은 다양한 단어를 사용해 보세요. 'very' 대신 'really(정말)'나 'extremely(극도로)' 같은 단어를 쓰면 느낌을 더 강하게 표현할 수 있어요.

Grammar

Beginner (Below G2)

학생은 여러 기초 문법 영역에서 도움이 필요합니다.
• 대문자 사용 (Capitalization): 문장의 첫 글자를 소문자로 시작하는 경우가 많아요('it is becase'). 모든 문장은 대문자로 시작해야 해요.
• 주어-동사 일치 (Subject-Verb Agreement): 'The hotel and the hotel's pool'은 두 가지이므로 'was'가 아닌 'were'를 써야 해요.
• 시제 일치 (Verb Tense): 과거의 경험에 대해 이야기하면서 현재 시제를 섞어 쓰고 있어요("The hotel that I went is Hilton hotel"). 모든 동사를 과거형으로 통일해야 해요.
• 철자 (Spelling): `becase`, `hamberger`, `delicous`, `visted` 등 여러 철자 오류가 보여요.

Sentence Structure

Beginner (G2)

대부분의 문장이 'It was...' 또는 'The... was...'와 같은 단순한 형태로 반복되고 있어요. 'Also,', 'but', 'so' 같은 접속사를 사용해 문장을 연결하려는 시도는 좋지만, 문장 구조를 더 다양하게 만드는 연습이 필요해요.

Essay Structure

Beginner (G2)

서론-본론-결론의 기본적인 형태를 이해하고 있지만, 각 부분의 역할에 대한 이해는 부족해요.
부족한 점:
• Thesis Statement (주제문): 하와이가 최고의 장소인 이유를 서론에서 명확하게 제시하는 주제문이 없어요.
• Topic Sentence & Unity (주제 문장과 통일성): 각 본론 문단이 하나의 중심 생각으로 통일되지 않고 여러 내용이 섞여 있어요(예: 세 번째 본론에서는 날씨, 시장, 호텔 이야기를 함께 하고 있어요).
• Concluding Idea (결론): 결론에서 본론의 내용을 요약하지 않고, 쥬라기 공원이나 파인애플 주스처럼 앞에서 언급되지 않은 새로운 정보들을 나열하고 있어요.

Part C: Writing Corrections

Sentence: "The best place I ever visited is Hawaii. it is becase the beach was so good."

Correction:

The best place I have ever visited is Hawaii. It is because the beach was so good.

'ever'와 함께 쓰는 경험은 현재완료 시제('have visited')가 더 자연스러워요. 문장의 시작은 항상 대문자('It')로 써야 하고, 'because'의 철자를 고쳤어요.

Suggested Improvement (G3):

The most memorable place I have ever visited is Hawaii, mainly because the beaches were incredible.

'Best' 대신 'memorable(기억에 남는)'을, 'good' 대신 'incredible(믿을 수 없을 만큼 멋진)'을 사용해서 더 풍부하게 표현했어요.

Sentence: "the pool had a big water slide and a small one. However, the two was all good."

Correction:

The pool had a big water slide and a small one. However, the two were both good.

두 개('the two')를 말할 때는 'was'가 아니라 'were'를 써야 해요. 'all' 보다는 'both'가 두 개를 가리킬 때 더 자연스러워요.

Suggested Improvement (G3):

The pool featured both a big water slide and a small one, and they were both very enjoyable.

'had' 대신 'featured(특징으로 갖고 있었다)'를, 'good' 대신 'enjoyable(즐길 수 있는)'을 사용해서 더 구체적으로 표현했어요.

Sentence: "In conclusion, Hawaii is the best place I ever visted. It is because of the beach, hotel, hotel pool, hamburger, and the war boat."

Correction:

In conclusion, Hawaii is the best place I have ever visited because of the beach, the hotel, the hotel pool, the hamburgers, and the war boat.

'visited' 철자를 고쳤고, 목록을 나열할 때 각 항목 앞에 'the'를 붙여주는 것이 더 자연스러워요.

Suggested Improvement (G3):

To sum up, with its beautiful beaches, fun hotels, delicious food, and unique attractions like the war boat, Hawaii is the best place I have ever been.

단순히 나열하는 대신, 각 항목을 꾸며주는 말('beautiful', 'fun', 'delicious', 'unique')을 추가해서 결론을 더 풍부하게 만들었어요.

Part D: Final Revised Essay

My Unforgettable Trip to Hawaii

(Introduction) There are many beautiful places in the world, but the best place I have ever visited is Hawaii. It was a very fun and memorable trip with my family. I believe Hawaii is a wonderful vacation spot because of its beautiful beaches, amazing hotels, and pleasant weather.

(Body Paragraph 1) First, the beaches in Hawaii were incredible. The sand was soft, and I loved that the water felt both cold and warm at the same time. My favorite memory was making sandcastles with my mom. We spent a long time together laughing and building a big castle. It was a really fun and special time at the beach.

(Body Paragraph 2) Second, the Hilton hotel where we stayed was fantastic. The best part was the hotel's pool, which featured both a big, exciting water slide and a smaller one. Even though our room was not very big, we had a great view of the beach, which was amazing to wake up to. The pool was right next to the beach, so we could easily go back and forth.

(Body Paragraph 3) Third, the weather in Hawaii was perfect for outdoor activities. It was hot, but that made it very good for swimming. We also visited a local market that was very big and interesting. We ate delicious hamburgers and tried fresh pineapple juice there. Being able to enjoy the warm weather made every activity more enjoyable.

(Conclusion) In conclusion, Hawaii is the best place I have ever visited. The combination of spending fun time at the beach, playing at the wonderful hotel pool, and enjoying the warm weather made it an unforgettable trip. From snorkeling to seeing many trees, every moment was exciting. That is why I think Hawaii is the best.
